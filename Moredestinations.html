Kyoto: City of Ten Thousand Shrines

Experience the blend of ancient culture and cutting-edge technology.

Top Attractions in Kyoto

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Iconic landmark offering panoramic city views and a glimpse into Kyoto's vibrant culture.

arashiyama and sagano

Serene Shinto shrine surrounded by lush forest, dedicated to Emperor Meiji and Empress Shoken.

Maruyama Park

Beautiful park blending traditional Japanese, English, and French garden styles, perfect for relaxation.

Nijo Castle (Nijojo)

Historic castle with beautiful gardens and intricate architecture, showcasing traditional Japanese craftsmanship.

Top restaurants in kyoto

Wagyu Ryotei Bungo

The wagyu at Wagyu Ryotei Bungo Gion tastes rich, buttery, and melt-in-your-mouth tender. It has deep, savory beef flavor with a clean sweetness from the well-marbled fat. Eating it feels smooth and luxurious — each bite is soft, juicy, and tasty without needing heavy seasoning.

Sushi Kizaemon

Sushi Kizaemon is a sushi restaurant in Kyoto’s Pontocho area known for very fresh fish and rich red-vinegar sushi rice. It offers flavorful, high-quality nigiri in a traditional and relaxed setting.

The Sodoh Higashiyama Kyoto

The food at The Sodoh Higashiyama Kyoto is Italian with a Japanese touch — think fresh, seasonal ingredients prepared in classic Italian styles like pasta, risotto, and meat or seafood dishes, but with delicate flavors and artistic presentation. The meals are rich and flavorful, focusing on quality ingredients and refined plating.

Gion Shinbashi Nakatani

Gion Shinbashi Nakatani is a high-end kaiseki restaurant in Gion, Kyoto, serving beautifully presented seasonal Japanese dishes with fresh seafood and local vegetables.

Tagoto Honten

Tagoto Honten is a traditional Kyoto kaiseki restaurant. The food is delicate, seasonal, and beautifully balanced, with fresh ingredients that taste light, fresh, and subtly savory, highlighting the natural flavors of fish, vegetables, and other seasonal items.

Kyoto Hyoto Shijo Karasuma

Modern Japanese with a cosy feel. Colorful plates and a mix of different small dishes. The food tastes comforting yet refined-savory slightly sweet, and nicely seasoned.

Top hotels to stay in kyoto

The Gate Hotel Kyoto Takasegawa

THE GATE HOTEL Kyoto Takasegawa by HULIC is a modern, stylish hotel in central Kyoto with river views, a historic‑building charm, and easy access to Gion and Kawaramachi.

Ryokan Motonago

Elegant hotel blending traditional Japanese design with modern luxury. Offers spacious rooms, a world-class spa, and exceptional dining options, all with breathtaking views of the city.

Hotel The Celestine Kyoto Gion

Chic urban retreat offering luxurious accommodations and personalized service. Located near Kyoto Station, it provides easy access to the city's top attractions while offering a tranquil oasis in the bustling metropolis.

Piece Hostel Sanjo

Contemporary hotel located in the vibrant Nishiki district. Offers spacious rooms, multiple dining options, and a convenient location for exploring Kyoto's nightlife, shopping, and cultural attractions.

Kyoto Ryokan SAKANOUE

Iconic luxury hotel offering spacious rooms with panoramic city views, exceptional dining, and a serene spa. Located in the heart of Kyoto, it provides a peaceful retreat while being close to the city's vibrant culture and attractions.
