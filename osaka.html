Osaka: The Kitchen of Japan

Experience the blend of ancient culture and cutting-edge technology.

Top Attractions in Osaka

Universal Studios Japan

One of Japan's most popular theme parks, featuring exciting rides, movie-themed attractions, and the famous Wizarding World of Harry Potter.

Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan

One of the largest aquariums in the world, showcasing marine life from the Pacific Rim, including whale sharks, penguins, and sea otters.

Osaka Shopping Guide

Osaka is a shopping paradise, especially in areas like Shinsaibashi and Dotonbori, where visitors can find fashion, souvenirs, street food, and local specialties.

Osaka Castle

A historic landmark originally built in the 16th century. Surrounded by beautiful gardens and moats, the castle museum offers insight into Osaka’s samurai history.

Top restaurants in Osaka

Shimmachi Adachi

A Michelin-starred restaurant offering refined Japanese cuisine with seasonal ingredients. Each dish is carefully prepared and beautifully presented.

Teppanyaki WAJO

A high-end teppanyaki restaurant where premium meats and seafood are grilled in front of guests for a fresh and interactive dining experience.

Sui Okazaki

An elegant sushi restaurant known for its fresh seafood and perfectly balanced rice. The atmosphere is calm and intimate.

NELU KORAIBASHI

A modern fine-dining restaurant combining creativity and precision. The dishes are artistic, seasonal, and thoughtfully prepared.

Chukashunsai Morimoto

A refined Chinese-style restaurant offering seasonal dishes with delicate flavors and beautiful presentation.

Tenboshi

A popular tempura restaurant known for its light, crispy batter and high-quality seasonal ingredients. The tempura is fried to order, ensuring a fresh and delicious meal.

Top hotels to stay in Osaka

Holiday Inn & Suites Shin Osaka

A comfortable and modern hotel located near Shin-Osaka Station, offering convenient access to transportation and spacious, well-equipped rooms.

HOTEL THE FLAG Shinsaibashi

A stylish boutique hotel located near Osaka's main shopping district. It offers modern rooms and a welcoming atmosphere for travelers.

Patina Osaka

A luxury hotel featuring elegant design, premium amenities, and exceptional service in the heart of Osaka.

Conrad Osaka

A five-star hotel offering stunning skyline views, spacious rooms, fine dining, and a luxurious spa experience.

Hotel Monterey Grasmere Osaka

A European-style hotel located in the Namba area, offering comfortable rooms and convenient access to shopping and entertainment districts.
