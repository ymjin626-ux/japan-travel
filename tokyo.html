Tokyo: The Heart of Japan

Experience the blend of ancient culture and cutting-edge technology.

Top Attractions in Tokyo

Tokyo Tower

Iconic landmark offering panoramic city views and a glimpse into Tokyo's vibrant culture.

Meiji Shrine

Serene Shinto shrine surrounded by lush forest, dedicated to Emperor Meiji and Empress Shoken.

Shinjuku Gyoen Park

Beautiful park blending traditional Japanese, English, and French garden styles, perfect for relaxation.

Senso-ji Temple

Tokyo's oldest temple, known for its vibrant red gate and bustling shopping street, Nakamise Dori.

Top restaurants in Tokyo

Narisawa

Creative, artistic Japanese food inspired by nature. Very fancy presentation, almost like artwork. The flavors are unique and layered-earthy,fresh,sometimes smokey, and very orignial.

Den

Fun and playful fine dining. Creative plating with surprising ideas. The taste is bold but balanced-rich flavors mixed with light,fresh ingredients. It feels exciting but still comforting.

Sushi Dai

Classic sushi sport with a lively vibe. Simple sushi counter style.Very fresh seafood,rich and buttery fish,slightly sweet rice-clean and satistifying flavors.

Sukiyabashi Jiro

Very traditional sushi. Simple,clean presentation. The sushi tates fresh, delicate and perfeclty balanced-soft rice with rich, high-quality fish. Every bite is smooth and precise.

Ishikawa

Elegant,seasonal Japanese food(kaiseki style).Beautiful plates with small portions. The flavors are light,refined and carefully balanced-fresh vegetables,tender meat, and gental seasoning.

Kagurazaka Dining

Modern Japanese with a cosy feel. Colorful plates and a mix of different small dishes. The food tastes comforting yet refined-savory slightly sweet, and nicely seasoned.

Top hotels to stay in Tokyo

Ritz-Carlton Tokyo

Luxurious hotel with stunning city views, elegant rooms, and impeccable service. Located in the heart of Tokyo, it offers easy access to shopping, dining, and cultural attractions.

Mandarin Oriental Tokyo

Elegant hotel blending traditional Japanese design with modern luxury. Offers spacious rooms, a world-class spa, and exceptional dining options, all with breathtaking views of the city.

Four Seasons Hotel Tokyo at Marunouchi

Chic urban retreat offering luxurious accommodations and personalized service. Located near Tokyo Station, it provides easy access to the city's top attractions while offering a tranquil oasis in the bustling metropolis.

Hilton Tokyo

Contemporary hotel located in the vibrant Shinjuku district. Offers spacious rooms, multiple dining options, and a convenient location for exploring Tokyo's nightlife, shopping, and cultural attractions.

Park Hyatt Tokyo

Iconic luxury hotel offering spacious rooms with panoramic city views, exceptional dining, and a serene spa. Located in Shinjuku, it provides a peaceful retreat while being close to Tokyo's vibrant culture and attractions.
